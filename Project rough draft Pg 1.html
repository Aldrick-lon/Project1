About Us

Welcome to Denard's Backyard BBQ Restaurant-Boynton! We're a local restaurant and sports bar located on North Congress Ave, across from Target. We are known for our famous BBQ ribs, freshly prepared dishes, daily lunch, dinner, and delectable desserts. From our Smokehouse Burger to our BBBQ wings in our signature sauce, our delicious menu variety offers something for everyone. We offer over 30 beer varieties, including foreign imports and craft brews. We have you covered any day of the week!
